Key Features

Everything you need to manage your device inventory efficiently

Device Management

Complete device lifecycle management from procurement to retirement. Track asset tags, serial numbers, warranty, and maintenance history.

Employee Management

Manage employee information, departments, and device assignments. Keep track of who has which device at any time.

Assignment Tracking

Assign and return devices with detailed history. Track condition, notes, and maintain complete audit trail of all assignments.

Issue Management

Report and track device issues. Prioritize problems and monitor resolution progress with complete issue history.

Reports & Analytics

Generate comprehensive reports on device inventory, assignments, and issues. Export data for further analysis.

Secure Access

Role-based access control ensures only authorized personnel can access sensitive inventory information.

About the System

The Airtel End User Equipment Tracking System is a comprehensive solution designed to streamline device inventory management across the organization. Built with modern technologies, it provides real-time visibility into device status, assignment history, and issue tracking.

Our system helps reduce device loss, improve accountability, and optimize asset utilization across all departments.
